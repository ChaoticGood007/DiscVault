# Initial Concept

DiscVault is a professional-grade, self-hosted disc golf inventory management system. It features a unique "Vault-first" architecture that allows you to manage multiple separate collections within a single unified interface.

---

# Product Definition: DiscVault

## Vision
DiscVault is the ultimate professional-grade, self-hosted disc golf inventory management system. It's designed to empower everyone from casual throwers keeping track of their bag to serious collectors with hundreds of rare discs.

## Target Audience
- **Casual Throwers:** Players who want a simple, effective way to manage their current bag and see what they own.
- **Collectors:** Enthusiasts with large, valuable collections who need detailed tracking of condition, plastic, and storage location.
- **Bag Builders:** Strategic players looking to optimize their bag composition and identify gaps in their lineup.

## Problem Statement
Managing a growing disc collection across multiple bags, shelves, and storage bins is difficult. DiscVault solves the problem of **Inventory Tracking**, ensuring users always know exactly what they have and where it's located.

## Success Criteria
- **Speed & Search:** Users can find any disc in a 1,000+ disc collection in under 3 seconds using powerful, intuitive filters.
- **Easy Migration:** Effortless onboarding via a smart CSV importer that automatically maps existing spreadsheet data.
- **Visual Clarity:** Providing a clear, data-driven view of a collection's strengths and gaps.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Interactive Charts:** A visual-first approach using stability and speed charts to help users understand their discs' flight characteristics at a glance.
- **Smart Entry:** A streamlined "Smart Entry" workflow for quickly adding new discs with fuzzy matching and auto-suggestions.
- **Vault-First Architecture:** Maintaining strict isolation between different collections (e.g., Main Bag vs. Collector's Vault) while allowing a unified search experience.
